About Us

Surrounded by shops and restaurants, this understated hotel is a 7-minute walk from the Anantapur Museum and an 11-minute walk from the Clock Tower. Relaxed rooms feature Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, along with minibars. Room service is available. Kids age 7 and under stay at no extra cost with an adult. Dining options consist of a vegetarian restaurant, and a restaurant/bar serving regional cuisine. Breakfast is available.
